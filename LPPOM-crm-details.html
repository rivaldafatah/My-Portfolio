QA Testing For LPPOM Digitalization (CRM Module / Apps)

Overview: The Customer Relationship Management (CRM) module is one of 10 modules within the microservices-based LPPOM Digital Platform. This module is designed to manage company leads and support the end-to-end lead lifecycle, from registration to conversion into potential business opportunities. Within the CRM module, users can create and manage leads, classify them based on their status (potential, offering, or closed), and assign leads across different roles to ensure proper handling and follow-up. Lead data originating from the HAM module—based on regional agent submissions—is automatically integrated into CRM for further processing. Once leads are managed and qualified, the system enables users to generate quotations that are directly integrated with the Quotation Order Management (QOM) module, ensuring a seamless and connected workflow across modules.

Result

Testing activities successfully ensured that all core functionalities within the CRM module operated as expected, including lead creation, lead management, status classification (potential, offering, and closed), and role-based lead assignment across users. Functional testing confirmed that each feature worked according to business requirements and supported the overall lead management process. Integration testing validated that lead data originating from the HAM module was accurately received and processed within the CRM module, ensuring consistent and reliable data synchronization. Furthermore, the system successfully supported the transition of managed leads into quotation creation, which was seamlessly integrated with the QOM module. Overall, the CRM module demonstrated stable performance, accurate data handling, and smooth end-to-end integration across modules, supporting an efficient and connected business workflow.
